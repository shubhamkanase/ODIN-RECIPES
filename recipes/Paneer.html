Paneer Recipes

Paneer has a high nutritional value, which is why it is believed that paneer is good for health and should be a part of your daily diet.

It provides instant energy as it is rich in calcium, selenium, and potassium. It also has high protein content, making it a good choice for a vegetarian diet.

Ingredients

  • 4.0 Cube(16.0 gm) Paneer
  • 6.0 Cube(30.0 gm) Capsicum
  • 6.0 Cube(36.0 gm) Red Capsicum
  • 1.0 Tbsp(14.0 gm) Nestlé A+ Curd
  • 1 Tsp(5.0 gm) Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Tsp(0.76 gm) Red Chilly
  • 2.0 Tsp(7.0 ml) Oil
  • 20.0 Ml(20.0 ml) Water

Recipe instructions (taken from the book)

  1. step:-1 For marination In a bowl, add 1 tbsp Nestlé A+ Curd, 1/2 tsp red chilly powder, 1/2 tsp haldi, 1/2 tsp garam masala, 1/2 tsp black pepper powder, 1/2 tsp dhania powder, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and 2 tsp oil. Mix well.
  2. step:-2 Now add 1/2 tsp salt and 1 tsp corn flour mixture, 20 ml water and mix into a smooth paste. Keep aside.
  3. step:-3 For tikka skewers Insert chicken and capsicum alternately on skewers, apply the marination well.
  4. step:-4 the pan with oil.
  5. step:-5 Roast till light brown or well cooked.
  6. step:-6 Serve hot with coriander chutney.
